# Rule: SmartFlow Brand

Source of truth for brand rules is `AGENTS.md`.

## Colours

- black: `#0D0D0D`
- brown: `#3B2F2F`
- gold: `#FFD700`

## Preferred stack

Tailwind CSS, Radix UI, Lucide React, Inter, JetBrains Mono, glassmorphism, Digital Circuit Luxe style.

## Brand must not override

- accessibility
- readability
- focus states
- disabled states
- semantic success / error / warning states
