Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Cancel anytime during the trial period with no charges.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards through Stripe. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! Just choose your plan and start using SmartFlow immediately. We include onboarding support with all plans.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional credits as needed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
